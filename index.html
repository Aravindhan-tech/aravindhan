Who I Am

Builder. Innovator. Athlete.

AXOMED is a student-founded health-tech initiative building smart medical monitoring using IoT + AI. Powered by ESP32 microcontrollers, vital sign sensors, and real-time dashboards for clinical settings.

Python C++ C ESP32 SQL Front-End IoT Embedded C++

I am a 15-year-old student innovator and founder from Chennai, currently in 11th Standard at Jaigopal Garodia Hindu Vidyalaya. My journey in technology started inside the Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL), where I first got my hands on microcontrollers and hardware prototyping.

Since 2022, I've competed in over 10 hackathons across district, state, national, and global levels — winning 1st Prize at Nexathon'25, earning INSPIRE MANAK recognition with a ₹10,000 government grant, and achieving Top-15 finishes in international challenges like ASTERHACKS and natHACKS.

My current flagship project is the Axomed Smart Patient Tracker — a hospital bed monitoring system integrating ESP32 microcontrollers with vital sign sensors and an AI-driven patient dashboard, designed to be affordable and scalable for Indian healthcare.

Beyond technology, I'm a dedicated Karate practitioner holding a Brown Belt II, having competed at the National Karate Championship 2023. Martial arts has instilled the discipline and resilience that drives everything I build.

"Age is not a limitation — execution is everything."
